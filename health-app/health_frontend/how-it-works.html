Process

From symptom to care
in three steps

The whole process — from describing how you feel to having a hospital address in hand — takes under a minute.

1

Describe Your Symptoms

Open the Detect page and type exactly how you're feeling in plain language. No medical terminology needed — write the way you'd explain it to a friend. Mention when the symptoms started, how severe they are, and anything else that feels relevant.

Tip: The more detail you include, the more accurate your result. "Severe headache behind my eyes with a fever for two days" gives far better results than just "headache."
2

Enter Your Location

Type your city, district, or neighbourhood into the location field. This is how JU AI finds medical facilities relevant to where you actually are. You don't need to enable GPS — a city name is enough.

Tip: Be as specific as you can. "Ikeja, Lagos" will return more relevant hospitals than just "Nigeria."
3

Review Your Results

Within seconds a results popup opens with everything you need — no extra steps, no searching. Here's exactly what you'll see:

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Is this a replacement for a real doctor?
No. JU AI is a decision-support tool designed to help you understand your symptoms and get to the right care faster. Always consult a qualified medical professional for diagnosis and treatment.
Is my data stored or shared?
Your symptom descriptions and location are sent to the AI model for analysis only. We do not store, log, or share your personal health information.
How accurate are the results?
JU AI achieves high accuracy for common conditions when detailed symptoms are provided. For rare or complex conditions, results should be treated as a starting point for a conversation with a doctor, not a final answer.
Does it work outside Nigeria?
Yes. The symptom analysis works globally. The hospital finder uses your location, so it will surface nearby facilities wherever you are.
